FACILITES

Hostel-Facility
The College has separate hostel facilities for Boys and Girls located in the college premises, which are at an affordable cost of Rs. 69,000/- per annum(Girls) and 72000/- per annum(Boys) for the A.Y 2019-20. Full time resident wardens are available to take care of hostellers and to maintain discipline. A standby power generator is available with full time attendant in the hostel to operate during power failures.


ATM


The college provides round the clock ATM (Automatic Teller Machine) facility for both the
students and staff members so that they can do withdrawals of money at any point of time without hassles.
It is located at a point where it is easily accessible to every individual in order to cater to the needs of
their monetary transactions.

Medical Facilities





A medical room is available in the campus during the working hours with all the requisite facilities and emergency medicine.
Dr Anand Kumar David, (ph: 8008557623) is a full time medical officer assisted by a trained nurse. Ambulance facility is also available at the medical centre.
The college also has a tie-up with Malla Reddy Hospitals for availing their facilities at concessional rates.


Stationary


CMRCET has a well stocked stationary point where materials like drawing sheets, drafters, aprons,
lab records, writing materials etc., with photocopying facility are available.
